Gary Franks

Photographer, Designer, Developer

Gary Franks is a multidisciplinary artist committed to growing Canada’s arts communities. Exploring the visual arts as well as music performance and production, Gary’s performances have been featured at many of the the capital’s arts and culture festivals over the past decade. His projects have toured the country, and he has contributed prints and photographs to group shows in some of the capital’s independent galleries. Gary transforms lessons and experiences from one discipline to the next, cultivating a cohesive voice that speaks across creative formats.
